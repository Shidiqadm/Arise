Asian Canadian Heritage Month

A Time for Reflection and Appreciation

Asian Canadian Heritage Month serves as a reminder of the important role that Asian Canadians have played—and continue to play—in shaping the social, cultural, and economic fabric of our country. From pioneering leaders and trailblazing artists to dedicated community organizers and compassionate caregivers, Asian Canadians have made significant contributions to all aspects of Canadian society.

Celebrating Diversity and Unity

We embrace and celebrate diversity as one of our core values. Asian Canadian Heritage Month provides us with an opportunity to learn about and celebrate the diverse cultures, traditions, languages, and histories of Asian Canadians. Through educational activities, cultural events, and meaningful discussions, we aim to foster understanding, empathy, and respect for all members of our community.

Get Involved

We invite all members of our school community to join us in celebrating Asian Canadian Heritage Month. Whether it's by attending events, participating in discussions, or simply taking the time to learn more about Asian Canadian history and culture, there are many ways to get involved and show your support.
